About Us

i-Lab Result is an online result monitoring and scheduling appointment website for public and private testing laboratory clinics. We aim to give you a more convenient way of setting up a scheduled appointment by just clicking on your computer without the hassle of taking a single step outside your homes. We ensure that your laboratory results will be safe, accurate, and delivered to you in an efficient way. Ever since we've experienced this pandemic, most of transactions are now being done online. We noticed how systems in different establishments changed as policies got stricter and people become more wary going outside. We, as developers, took that opportunity to come up with an idea and develop this website. We aim to deliver an easier and convenient possible way of transacting with your local laboratory clinic without the hassle of waiting in line and pondering when you will receive your results.
